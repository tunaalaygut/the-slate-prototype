The S.L.A.T.E. Dataset Standards

Hand Detector Training Dataset

Due date: 25.02.2020 12:30
Before using dataset-tools/video_framer.py module

Following questions need to be answered "yes" before you use dataset-tools/video_framer.py to pull frames out of your videos.

What to do if you answer any of the following "no"? Go back to work.

  • Have you recorded at least 10 videos? (5-10 seconds each.)
  • Have you cropped your videos to only contain the relevant parts?
  • Have you recorded plenty of videos in different conditions? (Different spots, different lighting conditions different clothes etc.)
  • Are your videos representative of our products real-world use?
  • Have you gone crazy enough to make sure that our model will see many different hand positions during its training to become robust enough to detect almost any hand gesture?
  • Is the dataset folder structured as decided?
  • Have you placed your videos under dataset/videos directory?
  • Are you videos named as yourname_#.mp4? (.mp4 extension is optional.)
...now go ahead and use dataset-tools/video_framer.py module with each of your videos
  • Does your dataset/annotations directory contain 350 images?
  • Are the hand images named like hand_image_#.jpg?
  • Are the images numbered in your own, designated, range? (e.g. hand_image_[351-700].jpg)
  • Are your image sizes <= 256KB? (play/experiment with -rf to get there.)
  • Scan the images and delete any image that is not necessary.
Now, open LabelImg
  • Have you selected the YOLO format?
  • Have you selected the correct input and output directories?
  • Have you specified default label as "hand"?
  • Get to annotating :)
After you are done annotating
  • Are the images and .txt annotations in the correct directory? (dataset/annotations)
  • Are all the annotations made in YOLO format? (There should be a .txt file for each image with a content like "0 0.53455 0.67756 0.78587 0.91883")
  • Have you been careful enough? (Have you selected all the fingers etc.)
Finally
  • Compress your dataset folder as a .zip file.
  • Name it yourname_dataset.zip
  • Upload it to Google Drive/SLATE folder.
  • Let others know you are done via WhatsApp CAMO Group Chat. DONE.
